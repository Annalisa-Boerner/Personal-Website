STUDIO DRINK

Studio Drink takes in any of the millions of artists and genres on Spotify (Spotify) then recommends a series of drinks to the user based on their music choice (The Cocktail DB). Please note that the Render server snoozes periodically and the homepage may take up to one minute to load content when first visited.

Includes toggles for alcoholic/non-alcoholic drinks, day/night mode front end, a profile that displays favorites and custom recipes, and a back end database that publishes Mixologist recipes right to the site.

JavaScript | PostgreSQL | Node.js | Express | React/Redux | Cypress | Material UI | PostgreSQL | RESTful APIs
